philosophy

Lexicon, as its definition narrates, is a tool (Language Lab) to improve one’s vocabulary & a set of meaningful units in a language. Vocabulary of Functional Words (words used in conversations of day-to-day life situations) has always been the weak point for many. This happens because of the obvious fact that they do not get any exposure

To listen to those words in a real life situation while people are talking & To understand their correct use in the appropriate context when situation comes

Unless human Listens, Vocabulary is not created. Without Vocabulary, its use is not possible (Speaking). Unless a word is spoken, it can’t be Read and Written. This is the sole reason why the LSRW pedagogy has been gaining momentum and almost all languages are being taught using it.

Further to add this, every language has its own set of Vowels, Consonants & Sounds. In simpler words, every letter and the word comprising of letters has its own Pronunciation. If a word is incorrectly pronounced, it is not understood by the listener. To gain the knowledge of correct pronunciation, one must listen to the Master’s Pronunciation and try to Imitate. (Like a baby imitates its Mother.)
